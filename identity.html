CORPORATE identity

Branding and identity work for a portfolio of e-commerce businesses — logos, type, colour, and the systems that hold them together.

Role
Brand identity design
Year
2017 — 2020
Type
Client work · Frooition
Output
Logo · Type · Colour · Assets

During my time at Frooition I worked with clients who needed a brand identity to sit alongside their e-commerce web presence. Briefs ranged from a single logo to new colour systems, typography, asset refreshes — and everything in between. The clients I worked with valued well-considered brand identity, and I was happy to give their businesses a consistent, professional look across all their digital platforms.

The process here mirrored the rhythm of their web-design projects: a short period of research, a phase of working through concepts, and then “working up” the strongest options to high fidelity. The client reviewed those concepts and we iterated together until the final result was something they were proud to take to market.

01 — Selected work Logo & mark

Concept & refinement

Each project began with sketch concepts informed by the brief and a quick competitive scan, then narrowed down to a smaller set of directions to refine. The aim was always to give the client a clear, considered set of options — not an overwhelming menu — and to make the rationale behind each one easy to talk through.
