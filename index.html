About

Hello! I’m Carl, a product designer based in Birmingham, England with 9+ years of experience working in the design industry. I focus on crafting intuitive, user centered digital products from concept to launch. Skilled across UI design, UX design, user research and rapid prototyping. I’m passionate about solving complex business and user problems through design thinking, collaboration, and continuous iteration. Over the past five years I have been fortunate enough to work with a tier one wall street investment bank (Goldman Sachs) and a multi national energy company (National grid).
